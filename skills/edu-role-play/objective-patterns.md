# Objective patterns

Objectives must be observable in the transcript. If a judge with only the transcript couldn't tell whether it happened, it fails the linter.

## Verbs that work

- **ask / elicit** — "Ask Sarah what her current CRM misses."
- **quantify** — "Get Sarah to share a dollar, hour, or headcount number."
- **surface** — "Surface a specific pain point Sarah owns."
- **secure** — "Secure a follow-up meeting with a date and attendees."
- **handle** — "Handle a price objection without dropping the list price."
- **confirm** — "Confirm the learner has authority to sign."
- **state** — "State the refund policy verbatim."

## Verbs to avoid (linter rejects)

- understand, know, feel, appreciate, be aware, familiarize, learn about

These describe internal states the transcript can't prove.

## Phrasing template

> `<verb>` + `<specific object>` + `<observable marker>`

> "Surface (verb) a pain point (object) Sarah owns (marker)."

> "Quantify (verb) the impact (object) with a dollar or hour figure (marker)."

## Objective count

2–4 objectives per role-play. Fewer than 2: not worth a full role-play. More than 4: scoring gets noisy on free models.
